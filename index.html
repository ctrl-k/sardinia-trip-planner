Dive Sites

Advanced-certified sites accessible from Sardinia's three dive clusters. Jamie + Alex (both Advanced OW).

Secca del Papa 1

15–33m Seamount Advanced OW

Consistently rated the finest dive site in the Mediterranean. Limestone pinnacles rising from a 40m seabed, covered in giant red and yellow gorgonian fans. Huge grouper, clouds of damselfish, barracuda, and jacks. Multiple route options around the main ledge.

Secca del Papa 2

28–45m Seamount Deep / Nitrox rec'd

Deeper extension of Papa 1. Features rare yellow-and-red bicolor gorgonians found almost nowhere else. Mobula rays, Mediterranean moray eels, yellow cluster anemones. Strong currents possible — experienced advanced divers only.

Relitto di Molara (Oued Yquem)

30–36m Wreck Advanced OW

230-ft WWII cargo vessel sunk by Dutch submarine O21 in 1941. Largely intact — the wreck looms out of the blue as you descend. Giant grouper and European conger eels inside. Saddled sea bream, red mullet, and snapper schools outside.

Relitto Omega

~30m Wreck Advanced OW

Wreck dive accessible to advanced recreational divers. Part of Tavolara Diving Center's extensive wreck catalog in the marine protected area.

Relitto Chrisso

~30m Wreck Advanced OW

Third named wreck in the Tavolara catalog. Three wrecks within close range makes for excellent multi-dive day planning.

Il Grottone

Variable Cave / Grotto Advanced OW

Large cave and grotto system off Tavolara island. Swim-throughs, cavern formations, and abundant marine life in the sheltered overhangs.

Secca delle Ancore

Variable Reef

"Anchor reef" — named for historical anchor finds. Part of the 26+ recreational dive sites offered by Tavolara Diving Center.

Operator: Tavolara Diving Center

Porto San Paolo

Est. 1985 · 3 boats (capacity 36) · 26+ sites
Certifications: PADI, TDI, TECREC, CMAS, SDI. Technical diving, Nitrox, rebreather try-outs. Discovered the "Lobster Wreck" at 75m. Phone: +39 0789 40360. Book in advance for July.

Lavezzi / "Grouper City"

24–30m Reef Advanced OW

Enormous, friendly grouper that follow divers for the entire dive — a truly unique experience. Also abundant snapper, sea bass, jacks, and barracuda schools. Located in the Bocche di Bonifacio National Park between Sardinia and Corsica.

Secca di Washington

6–30m Reef

Located in the channel between Spargi and Budelli islands. Three different routes including a classic loop around the main ledge. Rich in passages among rocks and small caves. Suitable for a range of experience levels.

Becu Caves

Variable Cave System Advanced OW

A veritable maze of passages and tunnels. Narrow corridors, caverns, and swim-throughs offering a unique navigation challenge. Advanced orientation skills required.

Clan Ogilvie Wreck

52m Wreck TECHNICAL

1882 English vessel (Stephen & Sons), sank in 1888. Completely covered in sponges and gorgonians. Grouper, snapper, lobsters, morays. Beyond recreational limits — technical diving certification required. Not suitable for Alex.

Loredan Wreck

~30m Wreck Advanced OW

WWII Italian cruiser sunk by torpedoes from submarine HMS Safari in 1943. One of the most beautiful shipwrecks in the Mediterranean. Largely intact except for torpedo damage.

Operator: Nautilus Diving Center

Palau

PADI 5-Star Resort · Est. 1994 · 2 boats · 40+ sites
Covers all Maddalena Archipelago, Palau, and Lavezzi/Corsica sites. TECREC technical diving, Nitrox. Small groups, advanced safety (emergency O2, first aid). Book well in advance for July.

Piscinas Shipwreck

~15–20m Wreck

18th-century English shipwreck with cannon, accessible directly from the Le Dune Piscinas beach. Rich aquatic flora and fauna. Hotel-arranged dives guided by marine biologists.

Cala Domestica

Variable Reef / Wall

Dramatic limestone cliffs and clear water along the Costa Verde coastline. Less-dived than the northeast — more of an exploration feel.

Grotta Niedda (via Ajoo Dive)

Variable Cave System Advanced OW

System of tunnels, caves, and canyons near Iglesias. Octopi, lobsters, shrimp, and corals. Operated by Ajoo Dive (PADI-affiliated), ~45 min from Le Dune Piscinas.

Operator: Hotel Le Dune + Ajoo Dive

Costa Verde

Le Dune arranges beach dives directly. For cave diving and advanced sites, Ajoo Dive in Iglesias (~45 min) is the go-to PADI center. The west coast is wilder and less dived — the diving is a bonus here, not the main event.

Car Rental

A rental car is essential. Pick up at Olbia (OLB) on arrival, drive the northeast for 2–3 days, then the ~3 hour scenic drive southwest to Costa Verde. Drop off at Cagliari (CAG) on departure. One-way Olbia→Cagliari rentals are widely available from major agencies. If Jamie departs from Olbia instead, coordinate a second car or transfer for the last day.
